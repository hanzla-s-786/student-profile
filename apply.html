How to Apply for Admission

Step Description
🔹 Create an Account Register on the portal and verify your email or phone number.
🔑 Login to Your Account Use your credentials to access the admission system.
📚 Select Offered Programs Browse and choose from the available programs.
📝 Fill Out the Application Form Enter personal details, academic information, and other required fields.
📜 View Merit List Check the merit list to see if your name is listed. If your name appears, proceed to the next step.
Click here to view merit list
🧾 Print Challan Form Generate and download the fee challan for payment.
🏦 Pay at Any Bank Deposit the required fee at any designated bank.
📊 Track Application Status Check the dashboard for application progress and updates.
